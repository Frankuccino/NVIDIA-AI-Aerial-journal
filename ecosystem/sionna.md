# Sionna Neural Radio Framework

**Purpose:**  
Sionna is an open-source **neural radio framework** for simulating wireless networks and training AI models for communications.

**Key Features:**

- Built for **link-level simulation**
- Supports **PyTorch and TensorFlow**
- Works with GPU-accelerated RAN for real-time AI testing
- Allows **AI model prototyping for 6G networks**

**Why It Matters:**  
Researchers can **simulate and train AI models** for RAN optimization before deploying on real networks, saving time and cost.
