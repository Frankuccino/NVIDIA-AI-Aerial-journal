# NVIDIA AI Aerial Overview

**Purpose:**  
AI Aerial is NVIDIA’s ecosystem for building **AI-native 6G networks**, combining GPU-accelerated RAN, neural radio simulation, and digital twin technology.

**Key Components:**

- **CUDA-Accelerated RAN:** GPU-based radio access network stack for AI-driven optimization.
- **Aerial Framework:** Provides building blocks for deploying AI workloads in telecom networks.
- **Sionna:** Neural radio framework for simulation and training AI models for communications.
- **Omniverse Digital Twin (AODT):** High-fidelity virtual environments to simulate and validate networks.

**Why It Matters:**  
Telecom networks are moving toward **software-defined, AI-native architectures**. AI Aerial enables real-time optimization, signal processing, and testing of 5G → 6G networks.
